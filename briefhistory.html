A Brief History of Firewalls

Firewalls were originally intended to help routers differentiate networks. As their complexity increased, they began to filter unwanted neworking traffic from usual traffic. Early pioneers of firwalls include Bill Cheswick and Steve Bellovin from AT&T Bell Labs. They developed one of the first commercial network firewalls
